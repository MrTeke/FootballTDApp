## Description
Stop the attackers before they score!

Football Defense is a fast-paced tower defense game where you build
your defensive line to stop waves of increasingly dangerous football
players from reaching your goal.

PLACE YOUR DEFENDERS
• Defender – Fast-firing, long range. Your first line of defense.
• Stopper – High damage, built to stop powerful forwards cold.
• Goalkeeper – The last wall. Massive damage, guards the goal.

SURVIVE 20 LEVELS
• 5 leagues from Amateur Cup to World Cup Final
• Each level brings faster, tougher, and more relentless attackers
• Manage your budget — every placement decision matters

STRATEGY IS EVERYTHING
• Cover all spawn points or attackers will slip through
• Save money for upgrades or spend early for protection?
• 3-star each level to prove you're unbeatable

Can you keep a clean sheet through the World Cup Final?
